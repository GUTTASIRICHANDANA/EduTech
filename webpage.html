About Us

Lucky Charms University

It was established in 1995 as Lucky Charms College of Engineering, which was upgraded to Lucky Charms College of Engineering Autonomous in 2006 by UGC, and was declared as a Deemed to be University in 2009 by UGC, MHRD Govt.of India. In 2012, as a Deemed to be University, the institution was accredited by NAAC with A Grade and later in 2018, was re-accredited by NAAC with A++ grade. In 2019, UGC, MHRD declared this institution as Category I Institution.
